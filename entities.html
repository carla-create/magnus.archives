Entities

Make your statement, face your fear.

The Entities, also known the Fears, the Dread Powers, the Powers, and The Things That Were Fear are small parts of a shapeless force of fear manifest that exist next to our reality. Their only goal is to invoke fear into anything that comes across them.

They manifest in and influence reality through phenomena such as objects (artifacts), people (avatars), animals/monsters, books, and places. these supernatural happenings are extensions of these Entitites; a way for them to reach through into our world.

The Powers have been classed into 14 (15 if including The Extinction) categories, though they often bleed into one another, as there are infinate fears and combinations of them present in the world (much like colours, but if colours hated you). their power and influence changes over time and place based on the amount of that fear in the world.

The Eye

  • Other names Ceaseless Watcher, Beholding, It Knows You
  • Represents being watched/exposed/followed, having secrets exposed,the drive to know/understand
  • Manifestations Eyes, security cameras, creature/figure keeping vigil/watching, libraries, books
  • Artifacts Security camera instruction manual, the hand mirror, rock eye
  • Avatars Jonathan Sims, Elias Bouchard, Gertrude Robinson, Jonah Magnus

The Buried

  • Other names Choke, The Centre, Too Close I Cannot Breathe, Forever Deep Below Creation
  • Represents Small spaces, suffocating, drowning, being buried alive, everything crashing down around/on oneself, being trapped without enough space
  • Manifestations Caves, dirt, financial issues, heavy rain, underground transport, tight spaces such as coffins
  • Artifacts The Coffin, The Box, DIG, Seven Lamps of Architecture
  • Avatars Enrique MacMillian, "The Governor”, Hezekiah Wakely

The Corruption

  • Other names The Crawling Rot, Filth, Flesh Hive
  • Represents Corruption, disease, filth, disgust, revulsion and things/beings that evoke such feelings
  • Manifestations Rot, mould, bugs, decay, infection, unhealthy love and companionship, the feeling of one's skin crawling
  • Artifacts The Tale of a Field Hospital, A Journal of the Plague Year, The Scalpel, The Syringe
  • Avatars Jane Prentiss, Timothy Hodge, John Amherst, Agape, Jordan Kennedy

The Dark

  • Other names Mr. Pitch, The Forever Blind
  • Represents The dark, the unseen, unseen creatures
  • Manifestations Creatures lurking in the dark, shadow figures/monsters, coldness, blindness, dark water
  • Artifacts The Dark Star, Dark Wardrobe
  • Avatars Manuela Dominguez, Maxwell Rayner, Robert Montauk, Natalie Ennis, Vardaan Darvish, Callum Brodie

The Desolation

  • Other names The Lightless Flame, The Devastation, The Torturing Flame, The Blackened Earth, The Ravening Burn, Asag
  • Represents Pain, burning, loss, senseless destruction
  • Manifestations Fire, burns, wax, destruction, heat
  • Artifacts “small book bound in red”
  • Avatars Agnes Montague, Diego Molina, Jude Perry, Arthur Nolan, Eileen Montague, Eugene Vanderstock

The End

  • Other names Death, Terminus, the Termination of All Life, The Coming End That Waits For All And Cannot Be Ignored
  • Represents Death
  • Manifestations Skeletons, the undead, dreams
  • Artifacts Death's Game Pieces, Book of the Dead, Catalogue of the Trapped Dead
  • Avatars Oliver Banks, Justin Gough, Nathaniel Thorp, Tova McHugh

The Flesh

  • Other names Viscera
  • Represents Fear from livestock bred for meat, realisation that we are simply meat and bones
  • Manifestations Meat, visceral imagery, meat-related industry,bodies being abnormally warped, or butchered
  • Artifacts The Boneturner's Tale, The Stalwart Hunters' Almanac, the Meat Grinder
  • Avatars Jared Hopworth, Angela, Eustace Wick, John Haan, Toby Carlisle, Tom Haan

The Hunt

  • Other names Blood
  • Represents being chased or hunted, becoming prey
  • Manifestations Predatory monsters, animal instincts and traits
  • Artifacts The Stalwart Hunters' Almanac
  • Avatars Alice "Daisy" Tonner, Robert Montauk, Julia Montauk, Trevor Herbert, Basira Hussain, The Admiral

The Lonely

  • Other names Foresaken
  • Represents Isolation, loneliness
  • Manifestations Fog, large/empty rooms, silence, suburbs, crowds of faceless people
  • Artifacts Lonely Painting, A Disappearance, The Boatswain's Call
  • Avatars Martin Blackwood, Peter Lukas, Mordechai Lukas, Conrad Lukas

The Slaughter

  • Other names None
  • Represents Unpredictable, unmotivated violence, pain coming suddenly
  • Manifestations Soldiers, people driven "mad with Slaughter," music that induces or warns of violence, war or murder
  • Artifacts Blood Money, Slaughter Gun, Slaughter Knife, a rusted train car that smells of blood, "slaughter book"
  • Avatars Melanie King, Alfred Grifter, Amritsar Ghosts, The Deserter, "The Piper"

The Spiral

  • Other names The Twisting Deceit, It Is Lies, It Is Not What It Is
  • Represents Madness, false reality, deception, lying
  • Manifestations Spirals, patterns and fractals, hallucinations, illusions
  • Artifacts The Pattern Rug, The Fractal Pot
  • Avatars The Distortion (Michael/Helen), Gabriel, The Man Who Wasn't There

The Stanger

  • Other names I Do Not Know You
  • Represents The uncanny, the unknown, the unfamiliar
  • Manifestations Beings/things that evokes an "uncanny valley" response (mannequins, wax figures, masks, taxidermy, etc.), theatres and performances
  • Artifacts The Calliope, Gorilla Skin
  • Avatars Gregor Orsinov/Nikola Orsinov, The Anglerfish, Breekon & Hope, NotThem, Daniel Rawlings, Sarah Baldwin, Wolfgang von Kempelen, Anatomy Students

The Vast

  • Other names The Falling Titan
  • Represents Heights, falling, large open spaces, insignificance and meaninglessness
  • Manifestations Void, wide-open spaces, falling, vertigo, infinity
  • Artifacts Ex Altiora
  • Avatars Michael "Mike" Crew, Simon Fairchild, Harriet Fairchild

The Web

  • Other names Mother of Puppets, The Mother, The Spider, The Great Spider, The Spinner of Schemes, The Hidden Machination
  • Represents Spiders, being controlled or trapped (often unknowingly), loss of autonomy, manipulation
  • Manifestations spider webs, Spiders, web-like patterns, puppets
  • Artifacts A Guest for Mr. Spider, The Web Table, kumo ga tabete iru
  • Avatars Annabelle Cane, Raymond Fielding, Emma Harvey, Neil Lagorio
