Characters

Make your statement, face your fear.

These first four main characters in The Magnus Archives are archivists from the Magnus Institute that are tasked with filing and digitally recording statements regarding abnormal happenings. Elias Is their boss and had limited omniscience over the institute and beyond.

People working for the Magnus Institute are inherintly tied to The Eye, as the institute was created to collect information for that entity. They are not, however, all avatars of this entity.

Jonathan (Jon) Sims

  • Voiced by Jonathan Sims
  • Main character, head archivis, primary statement recorder
  • Avatar of The Eye

Martin Blackwood

  • Voiced by Alexander J. Newall
  • Supporting character, Archival assistant, Jon's love interest (from season 5)
  • Avatar of The Lonely

Sasha James

  • Voiced by Lottie Broomhall
  • Supporting character, Archival assistant, mudered and replaced by Not-Sasha at the end of season one
  • Not an avatar

Timothy (Tim) Stoker

  • Voiced by Mike LeBeau
  • Supporting character, Archival assistant, sacrificed himself at the end of season three to stop The Unknowing (ritual of The Stanger)
  • Not an avatar

Elias Bouchard

  • Voiced by Ben Meredith
  • Supporting character, head of the Magnus Institute, Possesed by Jonah Magnus (the creator of the Magnus Institute) after The Change and killed by Jonathan Sims
  • Avatar of the Eye
