About

Make your statement, face your fear.

The Magnus Archives is a fictional horror podcast that released anthologies weekly. It follows an organisation called The Magnus Institute that aims to research and document the unexplainable and weird. Accompany Jonathan Sims- the Institute's head archivist, as he organises and records the supernatural statements that lurk within the archives. Each episode is initally self contained, but start to connect as the seasons continue. Listen to these statements and Jonathan’s supplemental work to learn the goings on of the Magnus archives and unveil a conspiracy that leads back to the Institution itself.

Individually, these episodic statements are disturbing. As they come together, however, they reveal a web of horrors around every corner. “As the archivists look into the depths of the archives, something starts to look back…” (Rusty Quill)
